ScoreSense's Purpose

ScoreSense is designed to help tennis players communicate the score without losing track. Often, tennis players forget the score due to the overwhelming amount of changes we have to remember. This will lead to disputes that are extremely difficult to backtrack and figure out who is the wrong. Our program is ideally designed to be displayed on a large screen for easy visibility, while also being accessible on computers for portability and convenience.
